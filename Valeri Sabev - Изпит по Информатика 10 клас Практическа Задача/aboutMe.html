My Hobbies

Sports
"Sport plays a major role in my life, it is not just a hobby, but a lifestyle! I have played sports professionally for the most part of my childhood, tennis specifically Although this is in the past it has thought me a lot of valuable lessons for life. Sports like windsurfing, kitesurfing or even normal surfing on the other hand, have been having a significant positive impact on me. They have not only motivated me to do better in every possible aspect but also connect me to the outside world which could be a struggle otherwise for a computer geek like me. All in all, sports has and always will be a big part of my life!"

Computers
"From an early age (8 years old), I've had the blessing to have a computer of my own. This made me always have a passion for them whether that would be programming, playing video games or even troubleshooting and fixing issues. All of this, allows me to build up skills which soon or later I will use in the real world to solve problems and get paid for it. So far, I am liking the path I have taken. It allows me to work from anywhere around the world thus giving me opportunities to practice sports like windsurfing further connecting me to the outside world. This creates a good balance meaning a healthy lifestyle!"

YouTube
"As kids, we all have these dreams of becoming our idols, turning what happens to be a hobby or passion into a lifetime job. Well, been there done that. So the YouTube dream. That was me, 14 years of age grinding videos and streams every day. After all, I wasn't doing that bad of a job, within just a few months I managed to make my channel explode and gather over two thousand subscribers and 50K views. I was getting donations and ad revenue left and right and for the most part I was having the time of my life. Eventually, I decided to take all my computer skills I had build over the years and go into the path of programming!"
